Panlasang Illongo in .Batchoy

The owner an illongo decent from the province of Negros Occidental. Since batchoy is not popular as arozcaldo in Payatas, the owner decided to sell Lapaz batchoy in order to share the illongo's taste and history in Payatas area.

We serve authentic Lapaz Batchoy with a price that meets the financial capabality of Payatas residence. Our shop is visited by many especially during weekdays and holidays.
